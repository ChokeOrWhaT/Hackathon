Flood Preparedness — Quick Measures

Short, practical tips to reduce risk before, during, and after floods.

Alerts & Evacuation

Follow official advisories, have a go-bag ready, and know your nearest elevated/safer route and shelter.

Power & Appliances

Switch off mains if instructed; move electricals & valuables above likely water levels.

Water Safety

Avoid walking/driving through floodwaters; they hide currents, sinkholes, and open drains.

Home Prep

Clear drains, anchor fuel tanks, and elevate key utilities (pumps, meters) where possible.

After the Flood

Use boiled/bottled water; clean and disinfect surfaces; beware of structural damage and live wires.

Community

Check on neighbors, especially elderly/disabled; share verified updates and resources.
